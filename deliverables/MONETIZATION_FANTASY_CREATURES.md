## 5. FRUSTRATION HOOKS (High-Conversion Moments)

### Primary Hooks (20-40% conversion)

#### 1. **Capture Failure** (Highest Conversion)
- **Trigger:** Creature escapes at <10% health
- **Offer:** Reroll for ₹39 or Master Ball for ₹149
- **Psychology:** "So close!" - sunk cost fallacy
- **Timing:** Immediate popup with countdown (30 sec)

#### 2. **Boss Battle Loss**
- **Trigger:** Defeated on final boss stage
- **Offer:** Continue for ₹49 or Revive Pack for ₹99
- **Psychology:** Loss aversion - don't lose progress!
- **Timing:** Death screen with "Continue?" countdown

#### 3. **Energy Depletion**
- **Trigger:** Energy reaches 0 during active play
- **Offer:** Refill ₹19 / Watch Ad / Wait 30 min
- **Psychology:** Momentum broken, want to continue
- **Timing:** Immediate with "You're on a roll!" message

#### 4. **Inventory Full**
- **Trigger:** Capture attempt with max creatures
- **Offer:** Storage +50 for ₹299 or Release creatures
- **Psychology:** FOMO on rare catch
- **Timing:** Pre-capture check with warning

#### 5. **Evolution Wait**
- **Trigger:** Start evolution (4 hour timer)
- **Offer:** Skip for ₹149 or wait
- **Psychology:** Impatience, excitement for new form
- **Timing:** Evolution start screen

### Secondary Hooks (10-20% conversion)

#### 6. **Gacha Disappointment**
- **Trigger:** 5+ common eggs in a row
- **Offer:** Lucky Egg ₹79 or Premium Ticket ₹199
- **Psychology:** Gambler's fallacy - "due for a rare"

#### 7. **Level Wall**
- **Trigger:** Creature too weak for new area
- **Offer:** Training Burst ₹99 or 24h Auto ₹249
- **Psychology:** Want to explore new content

#### 8. **Trade Failure**
- **Trigger:** Trade declined or no takers
- **Offer:** VIP for trading post or buy gems directly
- **Psychology:** Social frustration

### Seasonal Hooks (Event-based)

#### 9. **Limited-Time Creature**
- **Trigger:** Event creature appears
- **Offer:** Master Ball ₹149 or event currency bundle
- **Psychology:** Scarcity + FOMO
- **Duration:** 48-72 hour events

#### 10. **Leaderboard Competition**
- **Trigger:** Rank dropping near reward threshold
- **Offer:** Power boosts to climb back
- **Psychology:** Competitive drive

## 6. CONVERSION FUNNEL (Player Journey)

### Stage 1: Onboarding (0-5 min)
**Goal:** Teach core loop, avoid overwhelming
- Tutorial: Capture first creature (guaranteed success)
- Grant: 50 free energy, 10 essence
- Show: Energy system but don't limit yet
- **Conversion Event:** NONE (let them enjoy)

### Stage 2: First Session (5-15 min)
**Goal:** Hook on progression, introduce premium
- Unlock: First evolution (immediate, no wait)
- Show: Gacha system (free first egg)
- **Conversion Event 1:** First energy depletion
  - Offer: ₹19 refill OR watch ad
  - Target: 15% convert

### Stage 3: First Day (15-60 min)
**Goal:** Create investment, first purchase
- Unlock: Battle system, leaderboards
- Show: What other players have (social proof)
- **Conversion Event 2:** First boss loss
  - Offer: Continue ₹49 (first purchase discount: ₹19)
  - Target: 25% convert
- **Conversion Event 3:** Inventory full during rare encounter
  - Offer: Storage upgrade ₹299
  - Target: 8% convert

### Stage 4: Retention (Day 2-7)
**Goal:** Build habit, introduce subscriptions
- Daily rewards (escalating value)
- **Conversion Event 4:** Week 1 check-in
  - Offer: VIP trial (3 days free, then ₹399/mo)
  - Target: 20% trial, 40% retain

### Stage 5: Investment (Week 2-4)
**Goal:** Deepen engagement, whale identification
- Events, tournaments, collections
- **Conversion Event 5:** Battle Pass introduction
  - Price: ₹399
  - Target: 15% purchase
- **Whale Detection:** Players buying 3+ consumables/day
  - Target: Exclusive offers, legendary packs

### Stage 6: Long-term (Month 2+)
**Goal:** Sustain revenue, community building
- Seasonal content
- Trading economy
- **Conversion Event 6:** Collection completion push
  - Target: 5% whale conversion on legendary packs

## ETHICAL SAFEGUARDS

### Player Protection
1. **Daily Spend Limit:** Optional ₹2,000/day cap for minors
2. **Clear Odds:** All gacha rates displayed (e.g., "5% Epic chance")
3. **Value Guarantee:** No purchase under ₹50, minimum meaningful value
4. **Progression Path:** All content achievable F2P (time investment)
5. **No Pay-to-Win:** Premium creatures have F2P equivalents

### Transparency
- Real INR prices shown, not just Robux
- "You can earn this in-game" messaging on all purchases
- No dark patterns (fake countdowns, forced navigation)
