## Quality Rating Criteria

### 10/10 - Perfect
- Exactly matches vision
- Low poly (<500 tris)
- Cartoon style matches Roblox
- No broken parts
- Animation-ready

### 8-9/10 - Excellent
- Close match, minor tweaks needed
- Low poly
- Good style fit
- May need recolor

### 6-7/10 - Good
- Usable with modifications
- Acceptable poly count
- Style close enough
- May need retexture

### 4-5/10 - OK
- Needs significant work
- Higher poly acceptable for Legendary
- Major recolor needed

### 1-3/10 - Poor
- Broken or unusable
- Wrong style entirely
- Skip these

## Recommended Recoloring Strategy

Since finding 25 unique creatures in exact color schemes is unlikely, plan to recolor by element:

### Recoloring Priority (Easiest to Hardest):
1. **Slime/Blob types** - Simple color change on single material
2. **Golems/Stone** - Texture swap for earth tones
3. **Serpents/Dragons** - Scale texture color shifts
4. **Birds/Wolves** - Fur/feather color changes
5. **Complex meshes** - Multiple material edits

### Recoloring Tools:
- Roblox Studio Material Manager
- TextureID replacement via script
- Vertex color painting for simple meshes
